How do subscriptions work?

Anxiety Quest is free to try. Premium unlocks all themed sets, every guided program and unlimited daily dares. You can choose a monthly or a yearly plan — the yearly plan includes a free trial. Prices are shown in the app before you confirm.

How does the free trial work?

The yearly plan starts with a free trial. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, it converts into a paid subscription. You can cancel anytime during the trial and won't be charged.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not inside the app: open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, tap Anxiety Quest and choose Cancel. You keep premium access until the current period ends.

How do I restore my purchases?

Got a new device or reinstalled the app? Open the app settings and tap Restore Purchases, signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy.

Is my data private?

Yes. There is no account and no tracking. Your progress and journal stay on your device (optionally synced via your personal iCloud). See our Privacy Policy for details.

I found a bug or have a suggestion

Please email us — feedback genuinely shapes the app. Include your iPhone model and iOS version if it's a bug.
